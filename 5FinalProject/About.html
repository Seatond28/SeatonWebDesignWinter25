About Us

Our goal as an organization is to bring together pokemon lovers to have a good time at the convention. We want to have the biggest gathering of people buying selling and playing pokemon within the next few years as our popularity grows


As an organization we have had to size up our venue every year to accommodate everyone that wants to come. We have been holding conventions for almost 20 years hosting people from all over the world to come and enjoy Pokemon. The upcoming pokemon world championship will be the biggest convention ever held too date bringing thousands to come watch trade and collect.
