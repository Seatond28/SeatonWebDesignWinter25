About Trainers United

Trainers United is a dedicated community that brings Pokémon TCG fans together for exciting events, tournaments, and trading opportunities.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a welcoming space for Pokémon TCG enthusiasts to trade, compete, and celebrate their passion.

Vision: To become the largest Pokémon TCG convention, fostering a global community of trainers.

Goals:

  • Host annual conventions with exclusive card reveals.
  • Provide a platform for competitive play and learning.
  • Support collectors in expanding their Pokémon card collections.

Our History & Achievements

Founded in 2000

Trainers United started as a small gathering of Pokémon fans and has grown into a nationwide convention. Where people from around the world have come to show their passion for Pokemon.

Major Achievements

We have hosted over 20 successful conventions, featuring top Pokémon creators and exclusive card releases.
