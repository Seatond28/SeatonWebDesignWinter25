Collecting Cards

I enjoy buying and collecting pokemon cards. As I contiune to spend money on good value cards I want to send them in to a grading company to get them graded to show the true value and if I should keep them or not. One of the most used and trusted companys for grading cards is PSA. If I ever were to have value in cards I would like to start reselling them so I can fund to buy more cards and repeat the process over again. I started collecting pokemon cards around 7 years ago when I went to summer camp and saw everyone else showing off their cool and colorful cards. Even after I stopped going there I still enjoyed opening cards and the dopamine I get from it.
